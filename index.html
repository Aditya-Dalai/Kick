About Me

I’m Clarence "Kick" Buttowski, the fearless "Suburban Daredevil" from Mellowbrook. With my signature white jumpsuit and red helmet, I tackle daring stunts and extreme sports, always aiming to be the world's greatest daredevil. My can-do attitude drives me to push boundaries, and I never back down from a challenge. Supported by my best friend Gunther and often teased by my older brother Brad, I face every adventure head-on with my catchphrase, "Show time!"
